ROAD INFRASTRUCTURE INFORMATION 🚧

ABOUT ROAD CONSTRUCTION

Road construction is a fundamental aspect of civil engineering and infrastructure development that has evolved significantly over centuries. Roads support transportation, trade, safety, and connectivity across regions.

PROCESS OF ROAD CONSTRUCTION

The construction of roads involves surveying, planning, excavation, subgrade preparation, laying base layers, asphalt or concrete surfacing, drainage systems, and road safety measures.

TYPES OF ROADS

Roads include National Highways, State Highways, Village Roads, Main Roads, Concrete Roads, and Asphalt Roads.

IMPORTANCE OF ROADS

Roads improve transportation, economic growth, tourism, emergency services, and public connectivity.

FUTURE OF ROAD INFRASTRUCTURE

Smart roads, AI monitoring, IoT sensors, and autonomous transportation systems will improve future road infrastructure.
